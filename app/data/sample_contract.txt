COMMERCIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Commercial Lease Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on January 15, 2024, between:

LANDLORD: ABC Properties Pty Ltd (ABN 12 345 678 901)
TENANT: XYZ Retail Store Pty Ltd (ABN 98 765 432 109)

PREMISES: The leased premises are located at 123 Main Street, Brisbane, QLD 4000, comprising Shop 5, Ground Floor, with a total area of 150 square meters.

LEASE TERM: This lease shall commence on February 1, 2024, and expire on January 31, 2027, with an option to renew for a further three (3) years.

RENT: The base rent is $5,000 per month, payable in advance on the first day of each month via direct debit.

RENT REVIEW: Rent shall be reviewed annually on the anniversary date, adjusted in accordance with the Consumer Price Index (CPI) for Brisbane, or to market rent, whichever is greater.

OUTGOINGS: The tenant shall pay a proportionate share of outgoings including council rates, water charges, insurance, and maintenance costs, as detailed in Schedule A.

PERMITTED USE: The premises may only be used for retail sale of clothing and accessories. Any change of use requires prior written consent from the landlord.

ASSIGNMENT: The tenant may assign this lease or sublet the premises with the landlord's prior written consent, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.

TERMINATION: Either party may terminate this lease by giving 90 days' written notice in the event of a material breach that remains uncured after 30 days' notice.

SECURITY DEPOSIT: A security deposit equivalent to two (2) months' gross rent ($10,000) shall be held by the landlord.

NOTICE PERIODS: All notices must be provided in writing with a minimum of 30 days' notice for any landlord-initiated changes.

MAINTENANCE: The landlord is responsible for structural repairs and maintenance of common areas. The tenant is responsible for internal maintenance and repairs.

FORCE MAJEURE: Neither party shall be liable for delays or failures in performance resulting from acts of God, natural disasters, pandemics, or other circumstances beyond reasonable control.

REGISTRATION: This lease, having a term exceeding three years, must be registered with the Queensland Land Registry.
